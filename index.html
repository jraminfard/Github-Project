My Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is playing basketball. Since the age of 10, I have been on women's basketball leagues. Basketball is truly my passion and I can't imagine going a day without playing this amazing sport. I look forward to playing every day, be it 30 minutes or two hours. I have won many trophies for being a good team player and for my many years of dedication to this amazing sport!

My Favorite Food

My favorite food is definitely sushi and poke. It's funny because the first time I tried sushi, I didn't like the taste at all. Later on, however, the taste grew on me. I started going to sushi restaurants and trying new rolls and flavors. I then became obsessed with this new food and now I have it frequently (even though it is said that too much is bad for you).

My Favorite Movie

My favorite movie is definitely The Notebook. I remember when it first came out, I watched it in theaters at least five times. The movie has such a great meaning to it and lesson to be learned: do not go after money, rather go after the person that makes your soul happy. To this day, I still love this movie and it is truly a classic! I have bought the DVD so I can watch it whenver I wish at home!

My Favorite book

My favorite book is defintiely The Light Between Oceans. Tom is a World War I veteran who maintains a lighthouse off the shore of Australia with his wife Isabel, a woman desperate to have a baby. Her prayers are answered when an infant washes up on shore in a rowboat. Tom thinks they should notify the authorities but ultimately gives in to Isabel's wish to keep the girl.
